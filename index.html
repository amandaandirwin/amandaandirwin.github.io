The Bride

Amanda Shawna Boney

Amanda Boney is considered to be an inspirational, visionary and pioneering artist/designer. #Creative #Resourceful. She chose the path of costume designing for her love of dance, theatre, film and art. At the London College of Fashion she explored a host of techniques and have developed a wide body of skills for designing for performance. Working at the National Theatre, The Old Vic and the Academy Costumes Limited has exposed her to the task involved in wardrobe management. Fabric hunting, texture mixing and color exploration piques her interest at every new opportunity. Sports also fascinates her and when not doing rollerblading, horse back riding, or swimming she remembers ice skating in a synchronized team. She hopes that her engagement with various performance and art projects would enable her to explore more substantial ideas and provoke thought in the field of designing, technology and art.
